Welcome to Warriorz

#StayHome #StaySafe #MaskOnIndia

With zero years of experience we, people of Vellore Institute of Technology has designed a responsive website for diagnosing the pernicious disease which has impacted many lives all around the world.

The proposed model which is deployed in this site can be used by physicians as digital diagnosing tool. The system is designed to collect the chest x­ray images uploaded by the user and categorised the image into two classes. The model is designed and trained to diagnose Covid-19 within a short span of time.
